Paragraphs

I am a paragraph, gap line inserted after every </p>

No other block-level tags should be contained within paragraph tags, but inline tags can, and often are, contained within paragraph tags.
Everything in HTML is wrapped up in tags, where they have been omitted the browser inserts its best guess, quirks mode is to be avoided!

Nested Lists
